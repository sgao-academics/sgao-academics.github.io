About

I am an independent researcher working on causal structure learning, graph neural networks, and their applications in computational genomics and innovation economics. My work focuses on making high-dimensional causal discovery tractable through cluster-aware differentiable optimization.

I hold a degree from Chongqing Institute of Foreign Studies (重庆外语外事学院). My research is self-directed and self-funded, driven by the belief that rigorous causal methodology can bridge the gap between machine learning and scientific discovery.
