About

I develop computational methods for causal structure learning and gene regulatory network inference from high-dimensional biomedical data. My recent work focuses on adapting constraint-based causal discovery algorithms to single-cell transcriptomics through distribution-aware conditional independence testing. I also work on spatial econometric models of digital innovation and welfare policy.

I hold an undergraduate degree from Chongqing Institute of Foreign Studies. My research is self-directed. I believe rigorous methodology and thorough empirical validation matter more than institutional affiliation.
