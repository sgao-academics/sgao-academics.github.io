## About

Independent researcher at Chongqing Institute of Foreign Studies (CIFS), working at the intersection of **causal discovery**, **computational biology**, and **digital economics**.

Current research spans:
- **Causal skeleton discovery** from single-cell transcriptomics (scCausal — under review at *BioData Mining*)
- **causalscale**: production-grade causal discovery engine (CAGate → SSCAGate → Causal Transformer → LowRankGNN)
- **Digital finance & spatial innovation lock** (three-paper series submitted to *Journal of Digital Economy*)
